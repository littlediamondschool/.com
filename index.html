Our Programmes





Play Group

The playgroup programme, designed for children aged 1.10 to 2.9 years, promotes free play, social interaction, and cooperation. Applying parallel play and group activities, children get engaged with their peers while fostering a feeling/sense of sharing and learning.



Nursery

The nursery programme is tailored for children typically between the ages of 2.10 to 3.9 years old. It provides a structured environment that epitomizes independence, self-directed learning, and hands-on exploration through a variety of age-appropriate activities.



L.K.G.

L.K.G., also known as Lower Kindergarten, is a programme designed for children usually around 3.10 to 4 years old. It includes language development, basic math concepts, sensorial exploration, and gross skill development, building the base for academic progress.



U.K.G.

U.K.G., also known as Upper Kindergarten, is a programme aimed at children around 4.10 to 5.9 years old. It includes Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Arts, and Physical Education, providing a base for academic & social development for higher schooling.
